About

I am Tianxing Wu (吴天行), a final year undergraduate majoring in Automation at College of Automation , Harbin Engineering University .

My main research interest lies in robotics and computer vision. In particular, I’m interest in vSLAM, 3D Perception, Pattern Recognition, Multi-agent System and everything about intelligent systems. I love programming, data analyzing and mathematical modelling, as well as developing control systems in real world. New knowledge and challenging topics always make me excited!

I’m also curious about the signal processing and fusion problem of multimodal interaction between human - robot, robot - robot and real world - virtual world, since I believe intelligence can emerge from connections.
