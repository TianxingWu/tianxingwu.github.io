About

I am currently a PhD student at MMLab@NTU, S-Lab, supervised by Prof. Ziwei Liu.

Previously, I recieved my Master degree from School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Yap-Peng Tan. I got my Bachelor degree at College of Automation, Harbin Engineering University.

My research interest mainly lies in computer vision and deep learning. Currently I’m working on Visual Generation and Multi-modal DeepFake Detection.
